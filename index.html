One Fine Evening in Minneapolis

"Photographs are just light and time" Aza Holmes

Since I was young, I used to dream about being a great photographer somedays. When I was young, my father bought me a Sony Camera and I started taking photos in the streets and whenever we went out. Even inside the house, I used to take a lot of photos of my plants and when I got old, the smartphones' cameras became popular and to me, those are the opportunities to create some memories from the mechanical lens.
